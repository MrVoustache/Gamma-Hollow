# Gamma Hollow

Tired of mining boring overworld caves? Want endless caves, full of various landscapes with lots of resources? Travel to Gamma!

## The Gamma planet

Gamma is a scorched world with a hollow crust full of amazing features and ores. Its very deep network of caves extends to many hundreds of blocks high with biomes changing by layers. You can also try reaching the very harsh surface to observe the stunning eternal night sky (don't get burned, or watch for too long).

## Access

You can access this dimension when you are really willing to mine deeper! Well, you just need to build a deepslate bricks, nether-style portal and open it with a diamond pickaxe.

## Mining

Gamma caves possess very high ore density, and ore distribution varies with heights and biomes so you may need to explore all levels to mine all you could need.

## Compatibility

This mod is compatible with **Modern Industrialization** and its ores will also generate massively if the mod is installed. It is also compatible with **Biomes'O'Plenty** and adds glow-worms to the deep caves if installed.
